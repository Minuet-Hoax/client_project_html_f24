Physical Health Support

Maintaining your physical well-being is a key component of academic success and overall life balance. Explore these resources to ensure you're taking care of your body while navigating your educational journey.

Health Care

At the University of Michigan, students have access to a range of health services that prioritize their well-being. Whether it's medical care, dental care, or specialist services, the University is committed to ensuring that students can thrive physically.

University Health Service (UHS)

UHS provide integrated, evidence-based, and comprehensive clinical care. Access medical care, including routine check-ups, vaccinations, and specialist referrals. Learn more at UHS Website.

UM Dental Care

The School of Dentistry clinics provide care for all your dental needs. Receive professional dental care through the University's highly regarded dental school. Learn more at School of Dentistry - Dental Care.

Food & Nutrition

University of Michigan offers essential food support for students within the campus community. Students can access a variety of nutritious food options to ensure that everyone has the resources they need to maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Maize & Blue Cupboard

Food and nutrition is important in maintaining physical health. Maize & Blue Cupboard supports students facing food insecurity, providing equitable access to healthy and nutritious food. Learn more about their services at Maize & Blue Cupboard.
